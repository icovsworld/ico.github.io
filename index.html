bio
I am Federico Corbani, a 24 years old detail-oriented graphic designer with a background in computer science. I hold a bachelor’s degree in Interior Design from Politecnico di Milano and am currently pursuing a master’s degree in Communication Design. My design experience encompasses art direction, branding, editorial design, UI, and UX. As a producer and DJ, my main source of inspiration comes from the world of music and the captivating visual landscapes created to complement it, such as album art, live visuals, and club series. In general, I have a deep passion for anything with a genuine and carefully crafted story to tell through its visual appeal.
